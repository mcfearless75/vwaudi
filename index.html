Questions

Things drivers ask before they book.

Will an independent specialist affect my VW or Audi warranty?

No. EU Block Exemption Regulation means you can service your VW, Audi, SEAT or Škoda at any qualified independent specialist using approved parts without invalidating the manufacturer warranty. We stamp the book to manufacturer schedule.

How much can I save versus a main dealer?

Up to 70% on labour and parts depending on the job — without compromising on diagnostic kit, parts quality or workmanship.

Do you offer MOTs while I wait?

Yes. We have an on-site MOT bay since 2006 and aim to return your vehicle the same day where possible. Courtesy car available on request.

Can you do ADAS calibration?

Yes — static ADAS calibration on VW, Audi, SEAT and Škoda models. Required after windscreen replacement, suspension work or front-end repairs.

Where are you based and what areas do you cover?

Carsthorne Road, Carr Lane Industrial Estate, Hoylake, Wirral CH47 4FB. Customers travel to us from across the Wirral, Merseyside, Liverpool, Chester and North Wales.

What models do you work on?

The full VAG range: Volkswagen (Golf, Polo, Passat, Tiguan, T-Roc, Touareg, Transporter, Caddy), Audi (A1–A8, Q2–Q8, TT, RS), SEAT (Ibiza, Leon, Ateca, Arona, Cupra) and Škoda (Fabia, Octavia, Superb, Kodiaq, Karoq).
